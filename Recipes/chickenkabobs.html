Chicken Kabobs

Description

These tasty chicken kabobs are baked in the oven, then finished under the broiler. The result: juicy kabobs that everyone will love!

Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 20 minutes | Total time: 35 minutes | Servings: 4 Servings

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 2 large chicken breasts (12 oz each)

Steps

  1. If using wooden skewers, soak them in water for 20 minutes while you prepare the chicken.
  2. Preheat the oven to 450Â°F. Line a rimmed, broiler-safe baking sheet with foil.
  3. In a large bowl, whisk together the olive oil, salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and thyme.
  4. Cut the chicken breasts into cubes as shown in the video. Add to the bowl and toss with the seasoned olive oil.
  5. Thread the coated chicken cubes on skewers. If you use wooden skewers, place thin foil strips on their exposed edges to prevent them from burning.
  6. Bake the kabobs until cooked through, for about 15 minutes.
  7. Switch the oven to broil. Place the baking sheet under the broiler, and broil the chicken just until browned, for about 1 minute. Serve immediately.
