Bison Burger

Description

This recipe provides you with the right steps to cook your favorite wild game meat and combine it into a classic burger!

Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes | Total time: 25 minutes | Servings: 4 burgers

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground bison
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsely
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 cup Avocado Oil

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, use clean hands to mix the bison meta and spices together until well-seasoned.
  2. Form four patties, each weighing 4 ounces, 3 inches in diameter, and 1/2 inch thick. Make a dimple in the center of each patty.
  3. Preheat a grill, a grill pan, or a dual-contact electric grill over mediu-high heat. Spray or brush the grill surfaces with oil.
  4. For medium doneness, grill the burgers for about 4 minutes on each side or for a total of 4 minutes on a dual-contact grill. Serve immediately.
